ABOUT US

A all in one site for students, parents and teachers.

  • We are focused on putting students at the centre of their learning and allowing them to be co-creators of the experience.
  • We are committed to meeting all students where they are and engaging them in meaningful, authentic learning experience.
  • we believe students should feel like active participants in their learning,that they can expand their understanding of what is possible and learn to ask question not just accept answer.
  • We are focused to empower schools and any educational institutes to design technology driven courses and enlighten the idea of smart and virtual school among students.


Why bloom academics?
As we embark on the fourth Industrial Revolution, it's clear that technology will play a central role in nearly all aspects of our lives. Research by the World Economic Forum estimates that 65% of children entering primary school will find themselves in occupations that today do not exist.
At the same time, 90% of organisations currently have an IT skills shortage, while 75% of educators and students feel there is a gap in their ability to meet the skills needs of the IT workforce. To prepare the talented needed for the digital economy, education must adapt technology.So,here,we are!
